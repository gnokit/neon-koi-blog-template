About Neon Koi

Neon Koi is a modern, fast, and beautiful blog template built with Astro 5.x. Designed for developers, writers, and creators who value performance and clean design.

Why Neon Koi?

The name "Neon Koi" combines the vibrant, eye-catching nature of neon lights with the grace and tranquility of koi fish. It represents the balance between striking visual design and smooth, peaceful user experience.
